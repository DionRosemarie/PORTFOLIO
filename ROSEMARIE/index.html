ABOUT ME

When design meets interactivity

Visual communication is, in my opinion, the most reliable way of communication we have. Shapes, colors, composition, fonts can create emotions and submitted a message in a few seconds. The right combination of those components can make a difference in our way of seeing things. I have chosen to go in graphic design to develop my skills in creation. Projects after projects, I was never tired of them. When I finished my DEC, I wanted more. I wanted to push my abilities and creative skills further. I decided to go in Computation Arts at Concordia University to learn to programme. I have acquired a good base in graphic design, but I wanted to be able to create, and also produce my work. I wanted to understand the behind the scene to create more efficiently. For the past year, I have discovered the world of programming and how much possibilities we have with this language. Animation and interaction are now mixing with shapes and color to communicate.

Rose-Marie Dion
